Publications

Research contributions in agricultural biology, biocontrol, and microbial sensing

Membrane proteome analysis identifies key components of sensing in Phytophthora parasitica zoospores

Lupatelli, C.A. et al. | Scientific Reports 15, 23500 (2025)

This study presents comprehensive membrane proteome analysis revealing key molecular mechanisms underlying zoospore sensing in Phytophthora parasitica, an oomycete pathogen critical for understanding plant-pathogen interactions in crop protection.

DOI: 10.1038/s41598-025-08701-z

Automated high-content image-based characterization of microorganism behavioral diversity and distribution

Lupatelli, C.A. et al. | Computational and Structural Biotechnology Journal 21, 5640–5649 (2023)

Development of an innovative ML-based image analysis tool for automated monitoring of microbial behavioral diversity. This work demonstrates the application of high-content imaging and machine learning for quantitative characterization of microorganism interactions relevant to crop protection solutions.

DOI: 10.1016/j.csbj.2023.10.055

Integrative Proteomic and Biophysical Approaches to Elucidate Phytophthora Zoospore Sensing and Motility During Early Plant Interactions

Lupatelli, C.A. | Doctoral Thesis, University Côte d'Azur (2024)

PhD dissertation combining proteomic and biophysical approaches to understand the molecular basis of sensing and motility in Phytophthora zoospores during early plant-pathogen interactions. This research integrates laboratory and computational techniques to elucidate key mechanisms in disease development.

Conservation de la biodiversité (Biodiversity Conservation)

François, M., Jackson, L., Njekete, C., Lupatelli, C., et al. | Dictionnaire d'Agroécologie (Agroecology Dictionary), INRAE (2021)

Contributed chapter to the INRAE Agroecology Dictionary on biodiversity conservation strategies. This reference work synthesizes knowledge on sustainable agricultural practices and their role in maintaining ecosystem biodiversity.

DOI: 10.17180/vpgp-gc54
