About

R&D professional with 5 years of experience in sustainable crop protection, life science research, and funded innovation projects. My background bridges plant biology, microbiology, and data science, with expertise in regulatory compliance for biocides under EU Regulation 1107/2009.

I specialize in preparing registration dossiers and managing documentation for complex regulatory and scientific processes. Alongside this, I bring strong experience in project management, stakeholder communication, and cross-functional collaboration within international research environments.

My research experience combines molecular biology, biophysics, and proteomics to explore plant–pathogen interactions, complemented by hands-on skills in data analysis (Python, MATLAB) and scientific reporting.

I am a fast learner with a proactive and analytical mindset, motivated to drive innovation in the life science sectors.
