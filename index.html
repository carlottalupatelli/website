About

Regulatory Affairs and R&D professional with 3+ years of experience in sustainable crop protection, EU-funded projects, and scientific data analysis. Experienced in preparing registration dossiers under EU Regulation 1107/2009 and coordinating submissions and documentation with authorities. Strong background in agricultural biology and data analysis, with expertise in regulatory compliance for plant protection products and related inputs.

Currently based in Barcelona, Spain, working as R&D Product Manager at Biocontrol Technologies. Open to relocation and passionate about developing sustainable solutions for global crop protection challenges.
